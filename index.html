Our Objectives

To create financial incentives to encourage the public to dispose of their waste properly. Consumers can receive various incentives when they dispose of their waste, either personally or through their businesses, via our platform, Green Cycle Platform.

To support leading companies in achieving their corporate social responsibility (CSR) ambitions. Our platform can enhance CSR through statistics and performance reports on waste sustainability management.

Over 20 million tons of waste are sent to landfills in Kuwait annually. With your participation, we can reduce the amount of waste in Kuwait's landfills by 20% in 2028 and by 100% in 2040.

To Enhance awareness of waste reduction in Kuwait's landfills by issuing an international certificate for preserving Kuwait's landfills to every individual and institution participating in the Green Cycle platform.
